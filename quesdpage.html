Question : What i can do if my Car Battrey Froze?

────────────────────────

Answer : If possible, move your car to a warmer, insulated garage to allow the battery to thaw naturally and slowly. Avoid applying direct heat as rapid temperature changes can damage the battery.

Answer : Once the battery has warmed up to at least freezing point, you can try jump-starting your car with the help of another vehicle. This might give the battery enough power to start the engine, but be cautious, as attempting to jump-start a frozen battery can be dangerous if the battery is damaged.
